## ✍️ Description & Execution
### Leader
- Grab the hand of the follower behind you, palm up
- 123 - [[Enchufla]] with your follower, she goes inside the circle
- 567 - You go inside the circle
	- Keep the other hand it should go over your head
	- You turn to the left, your looking out of the circle
### Follower
- Give the hand to the leader behind you
- 123 - [[Enchufla]] go inside the circle
	- Keep the other hand, they should be crossed in front of you
- 567 - Leader goes inside
---
- Repeat until [[Enchufla]] or something is called to exit
- You are just doing the [[Diagonal Step]]
