## ✍️ Description & Execution
- It can be called at several points, let us assume we start with a [[Dame]]
### Leader
- 123 567 - [[Dile Que No]] 
	- Keep the hand of both the follower on your left and right
- 123 - Step into the circle
	- 3 - Let go of the follower on the left
- 567 - Give impulse to the follower, so she does a half turn and comes to your left
### Follower
- 123 567 - [[Dile Que No]] 
- 123 - Step back
- 567 - Half turn to the left
	- Keep the hand with the current leader and also give it to the one now to your left
---
- Repeat until something else is called
- [[Cadena]] can be called because everyone is already holding hands
