## ✍️ Description & Execution
- More dynamic than the usual, simple back and forwards
### Leader
- 1 - Step back (Left foot)
- 2 - Step right (Right foot)
- 3 - Bring left foot to the right foot
	- Right hand makes contact
- 5 - Cross in front (Right foot)
	- Push with right hand
- 6 - Back (Left foot)
- 7 - Back (Right foot)
### Follower
- 1 - Step back (Right foot)
- 2 - Step left (Left foot)
- 3 - Bring right foot to the left foot
	- Left hand makes contact
- 5 - Cross in front (Left foot)
	- Push with Left hand
- 6 - Back (Right foot)
- 7 - Back (Left foot)
