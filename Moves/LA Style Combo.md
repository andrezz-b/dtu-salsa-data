## ✍️ Description & Execution
- This is a long combo, you can break it up and use just parts of it
### Leader
- From [[Close Position]]
- 123 567 - [[Coca-Cola]]
	- You should kind of go with her, at the end you should be side by side
- 123 567 - [[Caminala|Camina]]
	- 1 - Cros back/front (in the same direction where she backsteps)
	- 23 - Go to the left
	- 5 - Cross back/front (important to keep moving and not on spot)
	- 67 - More on spot
- 123 567 - [[Vuelta]]
	- 78 - Toss left hand and grab the right
- 123 - Half turn
	- Ready for [[Coca-Cola]], right hand elbow to elbow with her left (or very close otherwise it doesn't work)
- 567 - [[Coca-Cola]]
	- Stop her on the left shoulder so she doesn't turn the whole way
	- You should end up a bit to the right, ready to go in front
- 123 - Go under the hand in front
	- 1 - Step forward, very slight lead for hear on the left to step back
	- 2 - Cross behind
	- 3 - You should end up again in elbow to elbow position
- 567 - [[Coca-Cola]] and stop
- 123 - Forward, but no cross
- 567 - 1 or 2 [[Vuelta]]
- 123 - [[Basic Step]]
	- 3 - Left hand should be up, and a bit back, but you should be pulling with both the left and right so she doesn't do a [[Vuelta]]
- 5 - Keep pulling
- 67 - [[Coca-Cola]] with roll in
- 1 - Tap for both, you should make her chest twist to the right, so it's towards the outside
- 23 - On spot/no step
	- You need to twist her to the left, preparation for right turn
- 567 - [[Babosa]] like turn, keeping the hand
	- You end in [[Hammerlock]]
- 123 - [[Enchufla]]
- 567 - [[Espejo]]
