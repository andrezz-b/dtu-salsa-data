## ✍️ Description & Execution
- After it is called the first clap is on 7
### Leader
- 7 - Clap by yourself, turning towards the follower on the right
- 1 - Clap with the follower on the right
- 3 - Clap by yourself, turning towards the follower on the left
- 5 - Clap with the follower on the left
- 7 - Clap by yourself, turning towards the follower on the right
- Repeat until something like [[Dame]] is called
- 7 - Last clap
- 123 - [[Dile Que No]]
### Follower
- 7 - Clap by yourself, turning towards the leader on the left
- 1 - Clap with the leader on the left
- 3 - Clap by yourself, turning towards the leader on the right
- 5 - Clap with the leader on the right
- 7 - Clap by yourself, turning towards the leader on the left
- Repeat until something like [[Dame]] is called
- 7 - Last clap
- 123 - [[Dile Que No]]
