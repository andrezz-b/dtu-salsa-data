## ✍️ Description & Execution
- Many of the standard can be executed on spot from [[Close Position]]
- Good for switching things up and breaking the usual routine
- Listed moves are ones which have some specific leads, but on spot works for [[Sombrero]], [[Vuelta]], [[Setenta y Uno]] etc.
	- Try out and see what works
## On Spot [[Coca-Cola]]
### Leader
- [[Close Position]]
- 3 - Twist, my left shoulder goes in, 
	- This causes follower to twist backwards (preparation for the turn)
- 567 - Turn the follower to my right
	- Opposite direction of [[Vuelta]], 
### Follower
- 3 - You should get a little twist (right shoulder goes back)
- 567 - [[Coca-Cola]] turn on spot
## On Spot [[Sombrero Doble]]
### Leader
- [[Close Position]]
- 123 - Get into [[Crossed Hands]] (change hands)
	- Prepare the turn
- 567 - [[Sombrero]] turn for her
	- Follower stays on spot, no walking
- 123 - Left turn for leader
- 567 - Another [[Sombrero]] turn for follower
	- [[Sombrero Finish]], you can end up more closer (90°) than traditionally where you are side by side (180°)

### Leader
- 1 - Push hand like in [[Enchufla]]
	- Can be high or low
- 2 Pull
- 3 - Turning motion
- 567 - Step back, keep hand around while she spins, so she doesn't go walking to the side
	- Light pull so she comes forward (while I am going back on 5)
