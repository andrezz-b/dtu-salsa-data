## ✍️ Description & Execution
- Similar to [[Siete]], but follower rolls in on spot
### Leader
- 8 - Tap preparation
	- You should be in front of the follower so she has no space to go forwards
- 1 - Push hand to the left
- 23 - Follower rolls in
	- Right hand should go on right shoulder blade
- 567 - Roll follower out
	- Lead by pushing with the right hand on the shoulder blade (left hand does nothing)
### Follower
- 1 - You should be stepping to the right not forward
- Left hand should be across your chest towards the right shoulder
