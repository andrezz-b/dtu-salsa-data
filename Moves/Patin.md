## ✍️ Description & Execution
- The direction of the switch is the same as [[Dame]]
### Leader
- 123 - [[Enchufla]]
- 567 - Walk into the middle, and put hands on shoulders making a circle
- 123 567 - Back step
- 123 - [[Opposite Vuelta]] towards the new follower
	- Follower starts the [[Dile Que No]] alone
- 567 - Finish the [[Dile Que No]]
### Follower
- 123 - [[Enchufla]]
- 567 - Go behind the leader and face the circle
- 123 567 - Back step, raise the same arm as the leg going back
- **Keep Eye Contact with your next leader**
- 123 - Start [[Dile Que No]] alone
- 567 - Finish [[Dile Que No]] with the leader
### Easy Version
Extends the Back step count by 4, leaders return with a [[Ronde]], the [[Dile Que No]] is started together
- 123 - [[Enchufla]]
- 567 - Walk into the middle, and put hands on shoulders making a circle
- 123 567 123 - Back step
- 567 - [[Ronde]] towards the new follower
- 123 567 - [[Dile Que No]]
