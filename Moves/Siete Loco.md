## ✍️ Description & Execution
### Leader
- 8 - Tap preparation
- 123 - [[Sombrero]] / [[Vacilala]] start, roll her in
	- No hand switch, raise your right hand
	- 3 - Weight should be on the left
	- You should be close and behind the follower (You should do some walking)
- 4 - (Optional) Tap
- 567 - Pull with the right to spin her
	- Raise the left up
	- You should do some walking to keep close and get to her side
- 123 567 - In [[Crossed Hands]] do [[Dile Que No]]
-  123 - Swing hands to the right (preparation for turn)
	- Very light so the follower doesn't turn
- 567 - You both turn, [[Espejo]] for leader
	- Keep connected
- 123 567 - [[Sacala]]
	- [[Corona Finish]] for her
- 123 567 - [[Sacala]]
	- [[Corona Finish]] for me
- 123 567 - [[Dile Que No]]
### Follower
- 8 - Tap preparation
- 123 - [[Sombrero]] / [[Vacilala]] start
	- Give your left hand to the hand the leader raised
- 567 - Around one and a half left turn
- 123 567 - In [[Crossed Hands]] [[Dile Que No]]
- 123 - Preparation
- 567 - [[Vuelta]]
- 123 567 - [[Sacala]]
	- [[Corona Finish]] on me
- 123 567 - [[Sacala]]
	- [[Corona Finish]] for leader
- 123 567 - [[Dile Que No]]
## Fancy Version
- Version in the video, a bit faster and harder to do
- Everything is the same until the part where you both turn
- 8 - Preparation, swing to the right
- 123 - You both do the turn
- 567 - Get her to the side, wrap your right hand around her
- Continue with the [[Sacala]]s
