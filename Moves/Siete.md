## ✍️ Description & Execution
- Similar to [[Panque]], but follower walks forward
### Leader
- 8 - Tap preparation
	- The follower should have space to turn/walk
- 123 - [[Sombrero]] / [[Vacilala]] like start, roll her in
	- No hand switch, raise your right hand
	- 1 - Pull forward
	- 2 - Raise and offer your right hand
		- No hand switch
	- 3 - Weight should be on the left
	- You should be close and behind the follower (You should do some walking)
- 5 - Push on the right shoulder blade to roll her out
	- Let go of the right after giving the signal
- 67 - Follower rolls out
### Follower
- 1 - Step forward
- 2 - Give leader other hand
- 3 - Roll in
- 567 - Roll out
