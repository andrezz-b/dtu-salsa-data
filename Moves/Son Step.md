## ✍️ Description & Execution
- Danced "off-beat" - 234 678
### Leader
- 8 - Right foot to the right (out of the circle)
- 23 - Left, Right on spot
- 4 - Left foot to the left (into the circle)
- 67 - Right, Left on spot
- 8 - Repeat from the start
### Follower
- Mirror what the leader does
- 8 - Left foot to the Left (out of the circle)
- 23 - Right, Left on spot
- 4 - Right foot to the right (into the circle)
- 67 - Left, Right on spot
- 8 - Repeat from the start
### How To Enter
- When doing Basic Step tap on 7 and step with the same foot the 8 of the Son
- Leader
	- 123 56 - Normal
	- 7 - Tap Right
	- 8 - Son step to the right, continue with Son
- Follower
	- 123 56 - Normal
	- 7 - Tap Left
	- 8 - Son step to the left, continue with Son
### How To Exit
- After the Son 8, step the 1 into the Salsa Basic
