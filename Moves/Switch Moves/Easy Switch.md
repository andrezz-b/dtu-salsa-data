## ✍️ Description & Execution
- This is a [[Role Switch]] move
- Counting will be done as normal, this may cause the counting to shift by 4 counts after a role switch
### Leader
- 123 - Normal [[Guapea]]
- 567 - Like [[Dame]]
- 123 - You are now a *follower*
	- You should be stepping forward (with the left foot) and doing the [[Guapea]] connection with the left hand
### Follower
- 123 - Normal [[Guapea]]
- 567 - Like [[Dame]]
- 123 - You are now a *leader*
	- You should be stepping forward (with the right foot) and doing the [[Guapea]] connection with the right hand
