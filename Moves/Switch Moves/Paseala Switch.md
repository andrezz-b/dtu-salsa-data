## ✍️ Description & Execution
- This is a [[Role Switch]] move
- Counting will be done as normal, this may cause the counting to shift by 4 counts after a role switch
### Leader
- From [[Dile Que No Position]]
- 123 567 - [[Paséala]]
- 123 - You become a *follower* and step back to start [[Dile Que No]] with the person on your left
- 567 - Finish [[Dile Que No]]
	- No count switch in this case
### Follower
- From [[Dile Que No Position]]
- 123 567 - [[Paséala]]
- 123 - You become a *leader* and step forward to start [[Dile Que No]] with the person on your right
- 567 - Finish [[Dile Que No]]
	- No count switch in this case
