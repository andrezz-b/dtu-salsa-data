## ✍️ Description & Execution
- Core of the move is twisting on 67, it can be started from several moves:
	- [[Dile Que No Position]]
	- [[Close Position]]
	- [[Prima]]
### Leader
- From [[Close Position]]
- 123 - Walk around the follower
	- Walk closely/fast to build momentum
- 56 - Keep walking
- 7 - Larger step, twist your torso (left shoulder going forwards, right shoulder going back)
	- You can push slightly with the hand that is on the shoulderblade
### Follower
- Keep a **very good frame**
- When the leader twists his torso it should feel natural to also twist
	- For styling during the twist drag your right foot in a half circle
